Con-cedar Transforming Your Dreams into Reality

CON-CEDAR INFRA PVT LTD is a premier construction company dedicated to excellence in delivering top-notch construction and infrastructure solutions. We are committed to delivering exceptional all-round construction services with a focus on cost, quality, innovation, and client satisfaction.

We started in 2012 as CEDAR INFRAS. We’ve grown to become a trusted name in the construction industry, known for completing projects on time, within budget, and to the highest standards of craftsmanship. With a diverse portfolio of successful projects in public and private sectors in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, it was time for us to step up. So we’ve rechristened Cedar Infras as CON-CEDAR (pronounced “Consider”) INFRA PVT LTD.
